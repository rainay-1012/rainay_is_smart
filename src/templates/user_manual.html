User Manual

Chapter 1: Logging In

  1. Visit vendosync.mystrikingly.com - Click the Sign In button and log in through your email or social media accounts.

  2. Logging In

    On the login page, you have multiple options to sign in:

    • Click "Sign in with Google," select your Google account, and allow necessary permissions.
    • Enter your registered email and password.
    • Log in using your social media accounts, such as Facebook or Twitter.

  3. Role Assignment

    By default, new users are assigned the Executive role. To change your role to Manager/Admin, email support at support@vendosync.com with:

    • Your Google email
    • Proof of payment

    Role changes are processed within 1–2 business days.

Chapter 2: Editing Profile

  1. Accessing Your Profile

    Click your user icon in the top-right corner to open the profile portal.

  2. Updating Information

    Modify fields such as your full name and phone number. Upload a profile photo (maximum size: 2 MB; formats: JPG, PNG).

  3. Non-Editable Fields

    Note that the following fields cannot be changed:

    • User ID
    • Email Address
    • Role

Chapter 3: System Features

  1. Vendor Management

    Navigate to the "Vendor" page from the sidebar. Click “Add Vendor” and enter:

    • Vendor Name
    • Address
    • Email Address
    • Category

    Save your changes.

  2. Item Management

    Go to the "Item" page via the sidebar. Click “Add Item” and fill in details:

    • Item Name
    • Category (e.g., Electronics, Office Supplies)

    Save the item into the relevant category. Once you've added the items you want to purchase, you can multi-select the items and click the Procurement Button to proceed with the procurement process.

  3. Procurement Management

    Open the "Procurement" page. Click on any procurement card to view its details, including:

    • The details of the list of items in the procurement order.
    • Suggested vendors for each item, along with their respective scores.

    Choose an item in the procurement order, select the suggested vendor for the item, and click “RFQ” to send a Request for Quotation to the selected vendors.

  4. RFQ (Request for Quotation) Management

    After initiating the procurement process, the system will send an email to the selected vendor(s) with a link to access the RFQ form. Vendors can fill in their accepted quotation details, and users can review, accept, and print the order.

    • Sending RFQ to Vendors: Navigate to the Procurement page, select the procurement order, and click RFQ to send a Request for Quotation to the selected vendor(s).
    • Vendor Fills in Quotation: Vendors can click the link in the email to access the RFQ form and fill in details such as quantity, price, delivery time, and additional notes.
    • User Reviews and Accepts Quotation: After the vendor submits the RFQ form, the user can review the quotation details on the Procurement page and click Accept to confirm the order or Reject to request a revised quotation.
    • Printing the Order: Once the quotation is accepted, the system will generate an order confirmation. Click Print to generate a printable version of the order confirmation.
    • Vendor Notification: After the user accepts the quotation, the vendor will receive a confirmation email with the finalized order details.

  5. Reports

    Only users with the Manager role can access the "Report" page. Navigate to the "Report" page to view procurement and vendor performance summaries.

  6. VendoBot

    Only users with the Manager role can access the "Chatbot" icon in the bottom-right corner of all pages. You can ask relevant questions to get insights or assistance.

Chapter 4: Troubleshooting Connectivity

  1. Basic Checks

    Ensure your internet connection is active. Restart your modem or router if connectivity issues persist.

  2. Clearing Cache

    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to refresh and clear cached files.

  3. Contact Support

    If issues persist, email support@vendosync.com with details:

    • Error message or code
    • Screenshot of the issue

FAQs

a. Technical Issues
Q: The system won’t start. What should I do?
A: Restart your device, ensure all required dependencies are installed, and check for updates. If the issue persists, contact customer support.
Q: Can I run the system offline?
A: Some features may be accessible offline, but full functionality typically requires an internet connection.
Q: How do I report a bug or issue?
A: Use the in-app support option or submit a ticket through the customer portal with details like error codes and screenshots.
b. System Requirements
Q: What are the minimum requirements to run the system?
A: The minimum requirements are:
  • OS: Windows 10 or macOS 10.15+
  • Processor: Intel i5 or equivalent
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Storage: 2 GB free space
  • Internet: Stable connection with 5 Mbps speed or higher
Q: Are there any specific hardware requirements?
A: While the minimum requirements are listed, for optimal performance, it is recommended to have a higher-specification system, such as:
  • Processor: Intel i7 or equivalent
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • Storage: SSD with at least 10 GB free space
c. General Usage
Q: Is there a user manual available?
A: Yes, a detailed user manual is included in the Help section or can be downloaded from the official website.
Q: Does the system support multiple users?
A: Yes, multi-user support is available with role-based access control.
